W. B. BERNARD

NO NAME: A DRAMA IN FIVE ACTS (1863)



No Name: A Drama

Note: With Wilkie Collins' blessing, William Bayle Bernard (1807-1875) adapted the novel No Name for the stage in 1863. It was never performed in England, although a limited number of copies were published to secure copyright.

I am indebted to Paul Lewis for access to the version published in New York by De Witt's Acting Plays, which is the source for this electronic version.
